This Privacy Policy describes how Einbürgerungstest 2026 BAMF handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is used.

📱 Information We Collect

Einbürgerungstest 2026 BAMF does not collect any personal information that identifies you, and does not require an account.

To understand how the app is used and to improve it, we collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics, such as:

  • which quiz modes are started and completed;
  • test results;
  • app settings in use;
  • anonymous technical information such as app version, device model, operating system, and general region;
  • anonymous error reports, to help us fix problems.

This information is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, or advertising identifiers.

🔐 Data Security

We do not collect personal data. The analytics described above are anonymous and aggregated, so they cannot be linked back to you. All quiz progress and flagged questions are stored only on your device.

👥 Use of TelemetryDeck to analyze app usage

We use TelemetryDeck to analyze usage data. The provider is TelemetryDeck GmbH, Von-der-Tann-Str. 54, 86159 Augsburg, Germany. This allows us to collect app usage data for the purpose of contract fulfillment on the basis of Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR.

What data is transferred?

The data collected is completely anonymized and does not allow any conclusions to be drawn about personal information. The following is collected:

  • an anonymized, untraceable user ID (per app installation);
  • actions defined by us (e.g. "quiz started", "test completed");
  • a rounded timestamp (to the nearest hour);
  • device metadata (e.g. system version, app version, device type);
  • additional metadata defined by us (e.g. selected state, translation language, test score).

What is not stored?

  • No IP addresses (not in logs, not in the database);
  • No cookies or tracking technologies;
  • No persistent identifiers that could be traced back to individuals.

TelemetryDeck's SDK is open source: github.com/TelemetryDeck. Further information on data processing: telemetrydeck.com/privacy and Privacy FAQ.

The app does not use advertising or cross-app tracking services.
